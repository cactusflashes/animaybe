This anime was… an interesting one. It’s a sweet story that highlights a gay relationship, with an action-packed plot. I will say on the forefront there are heavy trigger warnings, which are more so referenced in the context of the protagonist's backstory and not shown on screen, but there are some really hard moments to watch. It’s always been one of the most impactful anime I have ever seen. True story: I cried everyday for three (3) weeks after I finished this one! Joy will still cry every time we talk about this anime.
