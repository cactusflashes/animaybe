This is a tough one. I really want to say that I don’t have a deep appreciation for this one, but the poster and stack of manga in our apartment says otherwise. This is a strange anime for sure, but one of the best plots and unique animation I’ve seen. I’d describe this one succinctly as ‘edgy Zootopia’. The anime jumps right in with a lot of interesting taboos built into this universe and how their society handles them. Hint: furry racism. Seriously though, this is a great watch with some really interesting characters. It was also the first (and my all-time fav) manga I ever read!
