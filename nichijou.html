‘Nichijou’ literally translates to ‘ordinary life’ or ‘everyday life’. This anime follows three high school girls, Yuuko, Mio, and Mai. There’s not really an overarching continuous plot, as each episode has random clips compiled together that are extremely chaotic. A lot of the content is based on Japanese culture and traditions, so it can be a little confusing to follow at times, but believe me when I say you need to just hold on for your life. This is currently my #1 anime of all time.
