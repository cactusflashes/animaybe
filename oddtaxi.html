I watched OddTaxi as a simulcast back in summer ‘21. It was one of those shows that I found myself so annoyed when I’d finish an episode, since there was ALWAYS a cliffhanger. Luckily now all the episodes are out on CrunchyRoll. The anime follows a very introverted and awkward cab driver, Odokawa. It’s a murder-mystery with a pretty big twist at the end, which caught me pretty off-guard on my first watch. Each episode follows a different story & set of characters, and gradually begins to connect each piece of the puzzle together. It’s great.
